7-Day Gym Workout Plan

Day 1: Chest

Target the pectoralis major and minor muscles.

Bench Press

Decline Press

Seated Bench Press

Incline Dumbbell Press

Cable Chest Flys

Pec Deck Flys

Lever Chest Press

Pushups

Day 2: Back and Core

Target the latissimus dorsi and rhomboids in the back, and work on core muscles.

Ab Crunches

Rollouts

Bent-Over Rows

Lat Pull-Down

Pull-Ups

Seated Cable Rows

One-Arm Dumbbell Bent Rows

Machine T-Bar Row

Day 3: Rest

Rest day. Engage in active recovery such as biking or hiking.

Cycling

Running

Day 4: Shoulders and Traps

Work the deltoids and trapezius muscles.

Military Press

Machine Shoulder Press

Lateral Raises

Front Raises

Reverse Flies

Upright Rows

Dumbbell Shrugs

Cable Rotations

Day 5: Legs

Focus on the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and lower legs.

Back Squats

Deadlifts

Leg Extensions

Leg Curls

Front Squats

Good Mornings

Weighted Lunges

Glute-Ham Curl

Day 6: Arms

Target the biceps and triceps.

Seated Dumbbell Arm Curls

Skull Crushers

Cable Curls

Triceps Push-Downs

Preacher Curls

Triceps Extensions

Concentration Curls

Triceps Dips

Day 7: Rest

Rest day. Engage in active recovery, meal prep, or set up a training journal.
